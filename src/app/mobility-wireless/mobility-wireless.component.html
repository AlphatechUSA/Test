Mobility & Wireless Services

Alpha Technologies provides a comprehensive range of mobility services for our clients.

Mobile Devices Covered

  • Apple phones
  • Android phones
  • Tablets
  • Wireless Access Points

Process

  • Analysis - Alpha’s mobility experts evaluate organization’s goals in regards to mobile technology. We also evaluate needs for mobile device management & security. We then gather & document requirements
  • Solution – Alpha’s team facilitate device evaluation & recommended best practices
  • Deployment – We deploy the solution that meets the organization needs & which has been approved. This includes solutions for small groups of people or very large organization-wide deployments
  • Optimization – We complete a thorough analysis of the current state of the mobility program in place & provide recommendations to reduce costs, eliminate redundancy, increase efficiencies & enhance security

Support

  • Alpha provides remote & onsite support for mobile devices. Our mobile support program is based on Alpha’s Enterprise Service Management platform
