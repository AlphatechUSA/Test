Position: Market research analyst

    Posted 01/01/2018
    Address : Wilmington HQ

    Job Duties

    Identifying the underlying principles, reasons, or facts of information by breaking down information or data into separate parts. Using Structured Query language (SQL) extract data from spread across various tables in the database of CATS/HRMS and segregate the data for further analysis and create market segmentation, market profitability, competitor analysis reports, using strong analytical and data analysis skills and some basic SQL as well as ability to perform compiling, coding, categorizing, calculating, tabulating, auditing, or verifying information or data. Produce weekly, monthly and quarterly reports showing sales and activity reports. Use data cleansing and data interpolation techniques for missing data to construct new data points for analysis and report generation. Analyzing information and evaluating results to choose the best solution and solve problems. Use statistical techniques and linear regression to calculate key metrics and analyze data. Advice management based on the outcome and generates actionable business impact recommendations utilizing strong analytical skill and statistics knowledge. Identifying information by categorizing, estimating, recognizing differences or similarities, and detecting changes in circumstances or events. Participate in continuous improvement initiative by performing what-if analysis on multiple scenarios to mitigate risk, increase sales and competitive advantage using forecasting techniques. Monitor and forecast marketing and sales trends Measure the effectiveness of marketing programs and strategies Devise and evaluate methods for collecting data, such as surveys, questionnaires, and opinion polls Gather data about consumers, competitors, and market conditions Analyze data using statistical software Convert complex data and findings into understandable tables, graphs, and written reports Prepare reports and present results to clients and management

    Job Requirements

    Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Management or equivalent, Master's degree is preferred. Strong quantitative skill and mathematics knowledge. Keeping up-to-date technically and Using Relevant Knowledge. Keep up to date on upcoming technologies in existing and potential market segments to identify growth opportunities and provide recommendation to management. Using computers and computer systems (including hardware and software) to program, set up functions, enter data, or process information. Use SAP Crystal Reports Business Intelligence tool, Microsoft Excel macros, functions, pivot tables and PSPP (Opensource SPSS software) to perform data operations to prepare weekly, monthly and quarterly reports.
