Video conferencing has been around for many years, but thanks to advances in technology & falling costs, video has now become a mainstream means of collaboration for businesses of all sizes. The options range from free social platforms like Skype, to web conferencing platforms like GoToMeeting, room-based video at around $10,000 per room to equip, or telepresence rooms that can cost $100,000 or more to outfit. These options offer the ability to join meetings from mobile clients on smartphones, tablets & laptops.
