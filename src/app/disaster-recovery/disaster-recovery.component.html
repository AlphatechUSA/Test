Downtime can be avoided by eradicating single points of failure in your configurations. The capacity to recover improves when redundancy is added, but at the same time your costs rise. Alpha understands that DR is expensive, so choosing the correct resilient solutions based on risk tolerance & ensuring they are enhanced to meet recovery objectives is important. Our experienced team of Disaster Recovery Specialists are ready to help.

Disaster Recovery: DR enables the recovery or continuation of vital technology infrastructure & systems following a natural or human-induced disaster.

Business Continuity: It ensures an organization’s critical business functions continue to operate or recover quickly despite serious incidents.

Firstly, we will have the analysis phase where the client IT infrastructure will be examined. Then we will focus on the design phase where the cloud architecture design will be formed. We would then continue to the implementation phase to execute the proposal once approved. The concluding phase is the support & maintenance phase where the infrastructure is monitored & maintained.

Alpha’s Solutions Architect goes on site to analyze the client’s IT infrastructure. Initiative would be undertaken to study client’s existing IT environment & infrastructure which would include but not limited to servers, virtual machines, applications/software, entire collection of assets in production, & network.

As per the information gathered from the client’s on site survey (analysis phase), Alpha would incorporate that information & shall present various design scenarios to the client. These design scenarios would vary in cloud based disaster recovery architecture & associated costs while aiming for minimum downtime with high availability, fault tolerance, scalability & budget-effective solutions.

Alpha’s DR Specialists focuses on implementing the solutions approved in the design phase. We incorporate the items below based on clients’ needs.

  • Backup & Restore
  • Pilot Light
  • Warm Standby Solution
